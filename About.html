About Lukhanyo Sonkqayi

Lukhanyo Sonkqayi is a passionate advocate for healthy living and personal transformation. Based in Cape Town, he has made it his life’s mission to raise awareness around proper nutrition, physical wellness, and the importance of self-care.

With a heart for people and a drive to make a difference, Lukhanyo works tirelessly to educate and support individuals who are ready to change their lives for the better. He believes that true wellness starts from within — and with the right mindset and support, anyone can achieve their health goals.

Driven by Purpose

What sets Lukhanyo apart is his genuine care for each person he works with. He takes time to understand your goals, challenges, and lifestyle — offering not just guidance, but motivation and accountability along the way.

His dedication goes beyond routine coaching. Lukhanyo is committed to shifting mindsets, building confidence, and helping people become the healthiest versions of themselves. He leads by example, living the values he teaches and proving that lasting change is possible.

Through community engagement, one-on-one coaching, and powerful personal stories, Lukhanyo continues to inspire those around him to take control of their health and live with purpose.
